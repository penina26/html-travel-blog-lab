Adventures in Fire Island

My trip to Fire Island was the perfect mix of peaceful beach walks and coastal adventure. I spent the mornings hiking along the sandy dunes, listening to the waves crash and watching shorebirds glide above the water. The highlight of the day was climbing up to the Fire Island Lighthouse, where I had a panoramic view of the ocean, marshes, and endless stretches of shoreline. As the sun set, the sky lit up in shades of pink and orange, wrapping up an unforgettable day on this beautiful barrier island.
